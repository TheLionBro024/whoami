Origin & Language

Hey, I'm Adriel. I am based out of J.E. Estigarribia, Paraguay, in a mennonite colony called Sommerfeld, a place that has shaped my perspective in more ways than one. Growing up here means navigating different worlds and cultures daily. Because of that, communication is a huge part of who I am. I actively switch between English, Spanish, German, and Ploatdietsch, our Mennonite Dialect. Whether I am talking through a technical engineering problem or just connecting with people in my community, those four languages form the baseline of how I interact with the world.

Evolv.Platform & Engineering

When I am not in the office, I am operating under Evolv.Platform, my personal hub for bridging hardware and visual storytelling. I am a hobby videographer obsessed with high-dynamic-range realization and color grading. On the engineering side, I dream, design and hope to build, fine-tune custom carbon-fiber multi-rotor drones. From ArduPilot telemetry to writing code and designing embedded systems, I love the friction of making complex mechanical and software systems work together perfectly. It's a beautiful paradox because there are times where i feel like the engineering and the creative side cancel each other out. But in the end, I am learning to let the engineering guide my creativity and the creativity guide my engineering.
